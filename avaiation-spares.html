Aviation Spares

Aeromarineworld provides genuine spares for Bell Helicopters, Challenger planes, and Lockheed C-130 transport aircraft spares to customers in South Asia and can support AOG situations for any aircraft through our extensive network.

Aeromarineworld has been providing a wide range of high quality specification driven genuine spares from reputed US and Canadian manufacturers. We support the Textron Bell 206 and Bell 212 family of rotables and Cessna aircraft, Challenger Series 300 and 600 and Lockheed C-130 Transport aircraft, but can provide additional support to most airframes for customers in AOG situations. We source and export marine, aerospace and military specification products to private jet owners, maintenance companies and commercial airline clients.

We can provide spare parts using payment against Commercial Letters of Credit as well.

The Lockheed C-130 Hercules is a four-engine turboprop military transport aircraft designed and built originally by Lockheed (now Lockheed Martin). Capable of using unprepared runways for takeoffs and landings, the C-130 was originally designed as a troop, medevac, and cargo transport aircraft.
